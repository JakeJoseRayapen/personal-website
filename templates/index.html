My Coding Journey

Freecodecamp was where i started learning how to code. I did their first certifications(html/css) and gave me the oppotunity to work on projects(my first personal portfolio website and tribute page). I then went to their second certification(JavaScript Algorithms) and did some projects(calculator). Upon completing those, i realised that coding brought me a sense of purpose, i did not want my knowledge of it to be limited and felt that i should get a better grasp of the basics(C language).Hence,right after freecodecamp, i enrolled for the CS50(Intro to computer science). After that i collaborated with a friend for our final project(Casa Bitcoin) and then enrolled for Cs50web(Web programming with Python and Javascript). My plan for the future is to have strong fundamentals in python,statistics,algebra,probability and calculus before enrolling for Andrew Ng's machine learning course. I once watched a lecture about Artificial Intelligence and it has since then been my goal to try and learn more about AI. I might find another path if it turns out i dont like AI but for now that is my end goal.
